No Harassment

About

I am someone who has lived through violence and long periods of unfair treatment. This space is not about details, but about saying clearly that what happened was wrong and that nobody deserves to be treated this way.

Resilience

My life has been marked by fear, pressure and people ignoring my safety. Even so, I am still here and still talking. This page is a quiet signal to others who have survived harm: you are not alone and your story matters.

Letters

If you have gone through abuse, bullying or any form of harassment and want to share how you cope, you can write to me. You do not have to share names or private details. Simple honest words about how you keep going can help both of us breathe a bit easier.

Contact

You can reach me by email at artur.kse3@gmail.com. Please write only if you are respectful and understand that this page exists to support people who have survived harm.
